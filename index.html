How to use
Sky's Guide is a curated collection of places Sky personally loves — in the US and China.
1
Pick a city
Tap a city card at the top. Currently live: Bay Area, LA, Shanghai, Guangzhou. More coming soon.
2
Filter by vibe
Use 吃起来 / 喝起来 / 玩起来 / 住哪里 / 买买买 to narrow down. Tap again to clear.
3
Tap any card
Get Sky's notes, AI tags, links to Yelp / Google / 大众点评, and one-tap navigation.
4
Switch languages
Hit EN / 中 at the top right anytime.
5
Sky tested badge
Places with ★ and "Sky tested" have been personally visited and approved. Prioritize these.
Questions?
A few things people usually ask.
Is every place Sky-approved?▾
All places have been personally visited by Sky or recommended by people she trusts. Places with the "Sky tested" badge are ones she's been to herself.
How often is this updated?▾
Whenever Sky discovers something worth adding. No fixed schedule, just good finds as they happen.
Can I suggest a place?▾
Yes! Drop Sky a message. If it clears the vibe check, it'll make it onto the list.
What do the AI tags mean?▾
Tags are auto-generated by AI based on the venue info and Sky's notes — a quick way to scan what a place is about.
More cities coming?▾
Yes — Nanjing, New York, Tokyo and more are in the pipeline. Sky travels a lot, so the list grows when she does.
